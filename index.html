How do I get flags?

Click on any image for higher quality.

  • Open Discord DevTools
  • Go to network
  • In Discord, click on anyone and open their profile
  • In DevTools, look for "profile"
  • Click on it and go to the "Preview" tab and look for "user"
    • Note: None of this information is sensitive, I only blurred it out because it is not relevant.
  • Expand "user" and you will find "flags", those are the flags
